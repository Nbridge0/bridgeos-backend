Random old yacht archive.

Christmas 2015 decoration notes:

The main salon had a red and gold Christmas theme.
There was a medium-sized Christmas tree near the starboard window.
The tree had gold ribbons and red ornaments.
Pine garlands were placed around the window frames and cabinets.
Small candles were used on the dining table.
The dining table had white place settings and red festive napkins.
The aft deck had warm string lights in the evening.
